BUSINESS DESCRIPTION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Omega Technologies is a 40+ year-old specialist distributor
and limited manufacturer of precision aerospace and
industrial tooling. The company supplies blue-chip original
equipment manufacturers (OEMs) including Boeing, Lockheed
Martin, SpaceX, and Airbus with proprietary and third-party
tools used in aircraft assembly, maintenance, repair, and
overhaul (MRO) operations.

PROPRIETARY PRODUCTS:
  * Roller Ratchet(R) -- patented torque tooling system
    designed for confined-space aerospace fastening
  * SAVI(R) (System for Automated Vibration Isolation) --
    patented anti-vibration mount system for aircraft
    component installation

DIGITAL-FIRST SALES MODEL:
  * 80% of revenue generated through 23+ owned e-commerce
    websites with strong organic SEO
  * $4,300,000 in web-generated sales on only $45,000 in
    paid advertising (95:1 return on ad spend)
  * Amazon marketplace: 9% of revenue (one of 24+ channels)
  * No dependence on a single platform for majority of sales

CUSTOMER QUALITY:
  * Boeing:           ~6% of revenue
  * Lockheed Martin:  ~5% of revenue
  * SpaceX:           ~4% of revenue
  * Airbus:           ~3% of revenue
  * Largest single customer = 7% of revenue (low concentration)
  * 200+ active accounts; no customer exceeds 7% of revenue

SUPPLY CHAIN:
